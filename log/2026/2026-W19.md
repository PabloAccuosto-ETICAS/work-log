- **Methodological review of the Warden AI / Juicebox public bias
  audit (Wed May 06).** Noelia shared the report as an HR-AI
  assurance reference. Surfaced one structural limitation worth
  internalising for our reporting: the "group bias" test binarises
  match scores at the sample median and applies the 4/5ths rule,
  which is structurally insensitive to moderate group bias when
  scores are continuous and Ns balanced. The reported impact ratios
  of 0.95–1.00 across protected attributes are consistent with that
  test geometry, not with evidence of "no bias detected". The
  counterfactual analysis is more informative; its 95% threshold
  is generous. Logged as Key Finding #16 in the Wiselook
  INTERNAL_LOG. Confirms our reporting plan: raw 1–5 scores +
  non-parametric tests + ≥0.5 practical-significance threshold,
  framed as answering different questions, not as "better data".
  Drafted a note to Noelia summarising the four observations.
