- **v1.5.0: four IEEE standards added as external frameworks
  (Mon May 18).** Triggered by Alex's email of 2026-05-15
  (Pablo + Gemma cc). New frameworks: IEEE 7001-2021 (Transparency
  of Autonomous Systems, `compliance`), IEEE 7002-2022 (Data Privacy
  Process, `compliance`), IEEE 7003-2024 (Algorithmic Bias
  Considerations, `compliance`), IEEE 2894-2024 (Architectural
  Framework for Explainable AI, `reference` — IEEE Guide, advisory).
  Four category-level mappings on `bias-fairness`,
  `privacy-confidentiality`, `transparency-explainability` (×2).
  Counts unchanged (10/20/77); URIs stable; SKOS triples public
  517 → 525, internal 1736 → 1744 (+8 each, exactly 2 per new
  mapping). The 700x series went to `compliance` because each
  self-describes as conformity-assessable in its own text; 2894 went
  to `reference` because IEEE publishes it as a Guide, which is
  advisory rather than normative. All four mapped at category level
  only — the standards describe process methodologies cutting across
  whole categories (CoE CETS 225 / TC260 pattern). The
  IEEE 7003 mapping's `target_id` is `clause-7-and-9` matching the
  loci Alex highlighted; the citation broadens beyond her two
  sub-group illustrations to also acknowledge representational harm.
  Terminology fix on encoding: "Article" → "Clause" (IEEE's own
  vocabulary). [PR #22](https://github.com/Eticas-AI/ai-risk-taxonomy/pull/22),
  tag [`v1.5.0`](https://github.com/Eticas-AI/ai-risk-taxonomy/releases/tag/v1.5.0),
